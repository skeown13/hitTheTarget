Can You Make it to the Target!?!

1. Click on the 'Balloon', 'Burner', 'Rope', or 'Target' pictures in order to receive a Random Number (will be the same number throughout each game).
2. If you can add up your number to reach the Target Number exactly, then YOU HIT THE TARGET! However, if your number adds up to be more than the Target Number then YOU MISSED THE TARGET!

Good Luck!!
